Welcome

Albion Online is a non-linear mmorpg, where you write your own story without just following a set path. Explore a vast open world with 5 unique biomes, everything you do will have an impact on the world, with Albion's player-oriented economy, players create almost all gear from the resources they get, the gear you wear defines who you are, switch weapons and armor to go from knight to mage, or play as a mix of both classes. Venture into the open world against the inhabitants and creatures of Albion, start expeditions or delve into dungeons where you will find even more difficult enemies, face other players in open world encounters, fight for territories or entire cities in tactical battles, relax on your private island, where you can build a home, grow crops and raise animals, join a guild, everything is better when you work together. Enter the world of Albion now and write your own story.
